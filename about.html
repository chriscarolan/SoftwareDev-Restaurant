Marist Munchies

About Us

Marist Munchies has been serving the Marist community for over 10 years! Founded by four successful Marist alumni, our restaurant was made to add a fancy yet affordable dining experience to campus. In partnership with the Culinary Institute of America, we provide their students real life experience in our kitchens bringing some of the finest trained chefs, while also keeping our prices reasonable. With the Italian ambiance and the taste of the food, people feel like they are in Italy.

Marist Munchies became “the spot to be” very quickly. Families, friends, alumni, and locals all enjoy our restaurant. The restaurant is perfect for unwinding after a busy day, a first date, or even simply a fun time with family and friends. Our restaurant is now one of the most talked about restaurants on campus. Due to this, reservations often need to be booked a month in advance; however, we also reserve half of our seating for walks-ins on a first-come first-served basis.

Special Events

Pasta-Making Classes: Join us every second Saturday of the month for a hands-on pasta-making class from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM. Learn to make fresh pastas such as fettuccine, ravioli, or pappardelle, guided by a culinary student from the Culinary Institute of America. At the end of the class, savor your homemade pasta with delicious sauces and toppings or take it home for friends and family to enjoy.

Live Italian Music Night: Come dine with us on Friday nights to enjoy live Italian music. We bring in a live band or a solo performer playing classic Italian songs to create a fun atmosphere for dinner. Request your own Italian songs or join in and sing along for a night that takes you to Italy!

Italian-Themed Trivia Night: Test your knowledge of all things Italian at Trivia Night every Thursday night! Gather your friends and compete in rounds of questions about Italy's culture, history, cuisine, sports, and more. From art and landmarks to pasta types and Italian phrases, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Winners will walk away with exciting prizes, like gift cards or a complimentary meal, making it a night full of friendly competition and exciting rewards. Join us and be ready to eat, laugh, and learn a little more about Italy!
